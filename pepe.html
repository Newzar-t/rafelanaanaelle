Web Design Internship at Pépé Studio

Using Figma, Webflow, Photoshop, and Canva

During a two-week internship at a small French company called Pépé Studio, which specializes in services for healthcare professionals, I worked on several design and integration tasks. My missions included creating a new interface for the company’s website using Figma, integrating text and banners in Webflow, designing print materials for an association, and creating new content for social media. It was a truly enriching experience!
