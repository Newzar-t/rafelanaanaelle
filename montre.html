Vector Watch

Using Adobe Illustrator

For a school project, we had to choose a watch model and recreate it in Illustrator for large-format prints, cards, and more. As a big fan of video games, I was really inspired by that universe. So I chose a model that blends gaming and luxury: the Tag Heuer Chronograph x Nintendo, a stunning watch!

The entire illustration is vector-based, which means it can be printed in very high quality. Use the button below to switch between my reference image and my final design!
