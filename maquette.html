Creating an album for print

Using InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator

For a school project, the brief was to create a mock-up for a fictitious album, and the rest was totally free. At first, I wanted to make something really modern and trendy with a French rapper, but I also wanted to do something really offbeat, vibrant, and funny. Then I remembered the song about Princess Peach in the Mario Movie and chose to create something based on it.

Here are some of the designs I created, using Photoshop for all the photomontages, Illustrator for the logo, and InDesign for the layout.
