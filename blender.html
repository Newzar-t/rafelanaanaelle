Animation and 3D Modeling

Using Blender

For a school project, we had to create a 3D scene and animate it using Blender. I started from nothing and learned the basics of Blender in 5 days. I wanted to create a fun and interesting situation, full of color and life. I learned about cel-shading, parallax, and low-poly techniques to optimize the scene. Everything was made by me, except for the girl and her animation, which were downloaded from Mixamo.

This workshop was a great opportunity to learn a lot about 3D and gain a better understanding of how it works. It opened the door to many new projects in Unity and Three.js, so stay tuned!
